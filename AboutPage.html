About me

Mihaela Anton is a contemporary artist born in Romania in the 1990s. She discovered her passion for art in childhood and developed her talent through art studies at the local university, "George Enescu" in Iași.

Her style can be described as technical and realistic, as she enjoys using oil colors to reproduce what she sees. Some of the artists she admires include Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, Fabiano Millani, and Marco Grassi. Although she is just beginning to showcase her works at local and international exhibitions and galleries, every motivated artist has a place to shine.

Despite being at the start of her artistic career, Mihaela is constantly working in her studio, where she loves to paint and inspire others through her art.
